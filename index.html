VICONSYS introduces an advanced construction system enabling walls and structural elements to be cast up to any height in a single continuous pour — delivering unmatched strength, speed, and precision.

The Innovation

Traditional wall construction requires multiple lifts, joints, and repeated shuttering cycles. VICONSYS overcomes these limitations through engineered formwork and monolithic casting.

Proven on Site

A 24‑foot‑high RCC wall successfully cast in a single pour at the District Jail, Ahilyanagar.

The execution achieved excellent surface finish, vertical accuracy, and monolithic structural performance — validating the system under real site conditions.

Key Advantages

  • No cold joints (Monolithic structure)
  • Faster cycle time (Single pour)
  • Superior strength & durability
  • Reduced labour & handling
  • Ideal for Industrial & Infrastructure
